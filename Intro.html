You start your adventure in the office of a high-tech security company specialized in protecting important information.

You are a famous hacker known for your skills.

One day you decide to take up the challenge and try to break into the security system from another major company to prove that nothing is impenetrable.

However, you underestimate what awaits you.

Once you get inside, you realize that you have been targeted by a master hacker who hacked and stole your sensitive files.

A message from a mysterious hacker calling himself "EL CHAPO" appears on the computer screen.

He challenges you to get your own files back in a virtual environment that he made.

He devises a series of security puzzles and riddles that you must solve to unlock pieces of a code.

Once you get the code, you can get the stolen files back.

Little advice: Memorize every piece of code you get, there's a failsafe installed on the puzzles and riddles that kicks in the moment you leave.
